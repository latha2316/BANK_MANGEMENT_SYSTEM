Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account with People Go Bank?

To create an account, visit our Create Account page and fill out the required information. You will need to provide your account number, username, password, account holder name, account type (saving or current), balance, and optionally your PAN and Aadhar details.

2. How do I log in to my account?

To log in to your account, visit our Login Account page and enter your username and password. If your details are correct, you will be logged in and can view your account information.

3. How can I update my account details?

You can update your account details by visiting our Update Details page. Enter your account number, and the new PAN and Aadhar details you wish to update.

4. How do I make a deposit?

To make a deposit, go to our Transaction Details page, select the Deposit option, and fill out the required information including your account number and the amount you wish to deposit.

5. How do I withdraw money from my account?

To withdraw money, visit our Transaction Details page, select the Withdrawal option, and enter your account number and the amount you wish to withdraw.

6. How do I transfer money to another account?

To transfer money to another account, go to our Transaction Details page, select the Account to Account Transfer option, and provide the required details including your account number, the recipient's account number, and the amount to transfer.

7. What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, visit the Login Account page and click on the "Forgot your password?" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

8. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support by visiting our Contact page. We are available to assist you with any queries or issues you may have.

9. What are the minimum balance requirements?

The minimum balance requirement for a savings account is ₹500. For more details, please refer to our Create Account page.

10. How do I provide feedback?

We value your feedback! Please visit our Feedback page to share your thoughts and suggestions with us.
